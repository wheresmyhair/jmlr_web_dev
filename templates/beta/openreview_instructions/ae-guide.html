Overview

After being invited, you can find a link to your AE console under "Active Consoles" on the OpenReview homepage after you have logged into your account. Use this page to access JMLR committee consoles.

You could find all of the contents at the console, including recruit reviewers, assigned papers, etc.

Recruiting Reviewers

AE could recruit reviewers at the OpenReview console, or invite reviewers when assigning.

Approach 1. Recruit at console:

Click the recruit button and follow the instructions to recruit. The OpenReview system will automatically send email to the invitee.

Approach 2. Invite when assigning reviewers:

Note: The invitee will serve as a reviewer for this specific paper rather than become one of the JMLR regular reviewers. If you want to do so, please invite by recruiting.

View Available Reviewers

AE could browse the recruitment status of the reviewers at the bottom of the reviewer recruitment page.

Feel free to use the filters to sort out reviewers recruited by a specific AE.

After Receiving Review Tasks

After EIC assigns the submission to AE, AE should first do the review approval.

If AE submits the review approval with a "Desk Reject", EIC will receive another approval for this desk rejection.

↑ Back to top

Assigning Reviewers

After the review approval, AE could assign the reviewers through "Edit Assignments".

When the reviewer you want to assign is not on the list, try to uncheck "Show reviewers available for assignment".

Also, uncheck "Show reviewers available for assignment" to see the reviewers you have already assigned. Click the trashcan icon to undo the assignment.

Hint: You could speed up the assignment process by sorting the reviewer list in desending order.

Waiting for Reviews

AE will receive an email notification once all reviews are submitted. However, we recommend AE to check the review status regularly and notify the reviewer if necessary.

Proceeding with Partial Reviews (Force-Releasing Available Reviews)

By default, reviews are released automatically once all the reviews are submitted. In some circumstances (e.g., to bypass an overdue or unreachable reviewer), AE may need to proceed with partial feedback. This forces the release of all available reviews without waiting for the remaining reports. This function is triggered via an Official Comment.

Important Requirement: To trigger this function successfully, you must post the Official Comment directly on the Submission itself. Do not post this as a reply to an existing review or another comment; nested comments will be ignored by the system.

Instructions:

  1. Navigate to the specific submission page.
  2. Click the Official Comment button located directly under the submission title/abstract.
  3. In the comment form, scroll to the field labeled Action Editor Control Option.
  4. Click the input box to reveal the dropdown menu.
  5. Select: "Release all the reviews, regardless of how many reviews have been submitted."
  6. Submit the comment. The system will process this command and release all currently available reviews to the authors.

Rating the Reviews

Once all reviewers have submitted their reports and recommendations, the AE must rate the quality of each review. Please note that this step is mandatory; the decision button will not be available until all review ratings are complete.

↑ Back to top

Decision

After rating the reviews, AE could make the final decision on the submission.

The decision requires EIC's approval.

Camera Ready Verification

Please review the materials after the authors submit their camera-ready revision.

As part of this final check, please help us ensure the accuracy of the dates. Specifically, verify that the Year and Month in the paper header and the copyright footnote (page 1) correspond to the actual acceptance date.

Confirm the edits by clicking "Camera Ready Verification" and submit the verification.

Then the paper is officially accepted. The accepted edit will also appear in the revisions.
