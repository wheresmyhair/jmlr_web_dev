JMLR OpenReview Instructions (Reviewer)

Overview

After being invited, you can find a link to your reviewer console under "Active Consoles" on the OpenReview homepage after you have logged into your account. Use this page to access JMLR committee consoles.

You could find all of the assigned papers and tasks at the console.

Review

Start the review at the console.

Please follow the review instructions. The text area supports LaTeX formulas. For example:

You may want to submit your review in a PDF file. If so, please fill the text area with "See pdf below" and upload.

The review will be released after all of the reviewers finish their review.

Official Recommendation

Compliance Check: Please note that a mandatory compliance check is included at the bottom of the form. You must confirm that your review adheres to our standards before the recommendation can be processed.

After discussing with the authors, you may want to submit your final decision on this paper through official recommendation.

Optional Features

Official Comment

You could comment on almost every note and decision. Please comment correspondingly.

Remember to indicate the readers of your comment.
